Inkido is designed to be a private tool for learning. We collect only what is necessary to make the app work for you. User data is stored in [Supabase](https://supabase.com) — see their [privacy policy](https://supabase.com/privacy) for details on how data is handled at the infrastructure level.

## Data we store

- **Account Information**: Your email address is stored to manage your account and allow you to log in.
- **Learner Profiles**: Names of the profiles you create to track different learners.
- **Word Lists**: The characters and words you add to your spelling sets.
- **Homework Scans**: The summaries and extracted text from the worksheets you photograph. The original images are processed by AI and are not permanently stored on our servers after analysis.
- **Practice Statistics**: Records of your tone practice and AI usage to show you your progress and manage service limits.

## Analytics & Tracking

- **No Third-Party Analytics**: We do not use Google Analytics, tracking pixels, or any other third-party tracking services.
- **No Cookies**: We use local storage and session storage to keep you logged in and remember your preferences (like your active profile or theme), but we do not use tracking cookies.

## AI Processing

When you scan a worksheet, the image is sent to **[OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai)** for analysis. This is required to extract the text and generate sample answers. [OpenRouter's privacy policy](https://openrouter.ai/privacy) applies to this transient data processing.

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

- **Deletion**: You can delete individual words, spelling sets, homework scans, or entire profiles at any time within the app.
- **Account Deletion**: If you wish to delete your entire account and all associated data, please contact us at [inkido.foyer772@passinbox.com](mailto:inkido.foyer772@passinbox.com).
